Privacy Policy — Scrubbys Invoices

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Scrubbys Invoices is an internal tool used by Scrubbys Private Limited to create quotes and invoices for our cleaning services. This page explains what data the app accesses and how it's used.

What the app accesses

If you choose to use the "Import from Booking" feature, the app requests read-only access to your Google Calendar (scope: calendar.readonly) to look up upcoming bookings — event title, date, and location — so you don't have to retype client details into an invoice.

How the data is used

  • Calendar data is read directly from Google's API into your browser and is used only to pre-fill the invoice form you're working on.
  • The app does not have a server. There is nothing for calendar data to be sent to, stored on, or processed by, other than your own device and Google's servers.
  • Invoice and quote records you create are saved only in your browser's local storage on your own device — never transmitted anywhere.

What the app does not do

  • It does not store your calendar data anywhere outside your browser.
  • It does not share any data with third parties.
  • It does not modify, delete, or create anything in your Google Calendar — access is read-only.
