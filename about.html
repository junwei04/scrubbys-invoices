Scrubbys Invoices

Internal quoting & invoicing tool for Scrubbys Private Limited

Scrubbys Invoices is a private tool built for Scrubbys Private Limited, a home cleaning and handyman service in Singapore. It lets our team create and send quotations and invoices to clients directly from a phone, with PayNow QR codes for payment.

What it does

  • Creates quotations and invoices for cleaning services, with line items, discounts, and deposits.
  • Generates a PDF with a PayNow QR code so clients can pay instantly.
  • Optionally reads upcoming bookings from the user's own Google Calendar (read-only) to pre-fill a client's name, phone, and address when creating an invoice — saving manual retyping.

Who uses it

This is an internal business tool used only by Scrubbys Private Limited staff. It is not a public product or service.
